The tale is centered around Dr. Alexandre Manette and his daughter Lucie.  At the time the story starts Dr. Manette has just been released from an 18-year long prison sentence. Later in the story the reader learns that he was put in prison to suppress proof crime.

At the beginning of the book Dr. Manette is in a sore state of mind, but under the care of his loving daughter, Lucie, he slowly recovered his wits.

### Summary

This book confirms what I have been told, mainly, Charles Dickens was a great writer.  Although the build-up is a little lengthy, and it is hard to connect with any one the the main characters, Charles Dickens know how to make the punch line hammer! After finishing the book I was not sure whether to laugh or cry, so I re-read it!  _A Tale of Two Cities_ does have some long sentences, and is bit wordy, but for all that it is a must-read if enjoy historical-fiction.
